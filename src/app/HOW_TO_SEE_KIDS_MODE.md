# 🎨 How to See Kids Mode - Quick Start

## 🚀 **3 Simple Steps**

### **Step 1: Open the App**
Load the JotMinds landing page in your browser.

### **Step 2: Find the Purple Button**
Look for the **purple/pink gradient button** that says:

```
🎨 Kids Mode Demo
```

It's located in the hero section at the top of the landing page.

### **Step 3: Click It!**
Click the button and **instantly see Kids Mode** in action!

---

## 🎯 **What You'll See**

### **Welcome Screen**
- Colorful demo introduction
- Feature preview cards
- Big "Launch Kids Mode Demo" button

### **Kids Dashboard**
- Jot the mascot greeting
- Stats: Stars, Badges, Progress
- 3 colorful quiz cards
- Badge showcase

### **Try a Quiz!**
- Click any quiz card
- Answer emoji-based questions
- Hear audio narration (🔊 icon)
- See celebration at the end

### **Test Features**
- ⭐ Earn stars
- 🏆 Unlock badges
- 🎤 Audio narration
- 🔒 PIN protection (PIN: 1234)

## 🐛 **Troubleshooting**

### **Can't find the button?**
- Scroll to top of landing page
- Look in the main hero section
- Button is purple/pink with emoji 🎨

### **Audio not working?**
- Click on the page first
- Check volume settings
- Try a different browser
- Click speaker icon 🔊

### **Want to exit?**
- Click logout button (top right)
- Enter PIN: `1234`
- Returns to demo screen
