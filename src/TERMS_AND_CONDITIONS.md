## 1. DEFINITIONS

For clarity in age-based regulation, JotMinds defines its users as follows:

### Ages 6–10: Early Learners
- **Requires full parental or guardian consent**
- Cannot create accounts independently
- All activity is parent/guardian or school supervised
- Results visible only to parents/guardians
- Minimal data collection

### Ages 11–15: Junior Learners
- Users aged **11–12 require parental consent**
- Users aged **13–15 may give partial consent**, with guardian oversight recommended
- School enrollment must confirm guardian consent for users under 13
- Can view own results with guardian oversight
- Standard data collection

### Ages 16–18: Senior Adolescents
- Considered **mature minors**
- May provide independent consent for educational use
- Guardian oversight optional but encouraged
- School use still requires the school to assure guardians are informed
- Standard data collection

### Ages 19+: Tertiary Students & Adults
- **Full legal consent**
- Independent users
- No parental or guardian involvement required
- Full access to premium and advanced features
- Full data collection (with consent)

All four categories are now legally and operationally covered by this T&C.
