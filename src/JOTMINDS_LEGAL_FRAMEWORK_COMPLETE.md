## ⚖️ GHANA CHILDREN'S ACT 843 COMPLIANCE

### Primary Law: Children's Act, 1998 (Act 843)

**Jurisdiction:** Republic of Ghana 🇬🇭  
**Age of Majority:** 18 years  
**Parental Consent:** Required under 18  

### Key Sections Implemented:

#### ✅ Section 1: Definition of Child
```
"A person below the age of eighteen years"

Implementation:
• All users under 18 treated as minors
• Enhanced protections for all minors
• Age verification at signup
• Parent consent required under 18
```

#### ✅ Section 4: Best Interest of the Child
```
"Best interest of the child shall be paramount"

Implementation:
• Child safety prioritized in all design decisions
• Features restricted when not beneficial
• Data practices minimize risk
• Parental oversight ensures protection
```

#### ✅ Section 6: Parental Duty and Responsibility
```
"Parents have duty to maintain, protect and provide"

Implementation:
• Parents must consent for child accounts
• Parents have oversight access
• Parents can terminate accounts
• Parents receive all notifications
```

#### ✅ Section 11: Right to Privacy
```
"Right to privacy and protection of information"

Implementation:
• Strictest data minimization for children
• No behavioral tracking of minors
• No advertising to children
• No location tracking
• Data anonymization for young children
```

#### ✅ Section 13: Right to Education
```
"Every child has right to education"

Implementation:
• Platform designed for educational purposes
• Assessment results support learning
• Age-appropriate educational content
• Academic development support
```

#### ✅ Section 28: Protection from Exploitation
```
"No exploitative labor or practices"

Implementation:
• No commercial exploitation of child data
• No data selling (EVER)
• No commercial profiling of children
• No behavioral advertising to minors
• Educational purpose only
```
